⚡ 5 Bonus Engineering Challenges

01

The Invisible Umbrella

Design a way to keep a person dry from rain without using any fabric or traditional umbrella shape. What forces of nature can you use instead?

TRIZ Tool: Inversion
02

The Floating Library

A library needs to serve 3 different towns but can only exist in one place. Redesign it so it can serve all 3 towns without moving people.

TRIZ Tool: Segmentation
03

The Zero-Space Chair

Invent a chair that takes up zero space when not in use, but is fully comfortable when someone sits. No folding allowed.

TRIZ Tool: Nesting
04

The Noise-Eating Wall

A school next to a busy road is too noisy. Design a wall that makes noise disappear instead of just blocking it.

TRIZ Tool: Counteraction
05

The Self-Cleaning Shoe

Design a shoe that cleans itself while walking. It cannot use electricity or batteries. What material or mechanism would you use?

TRIZ Tool: Self-Service
